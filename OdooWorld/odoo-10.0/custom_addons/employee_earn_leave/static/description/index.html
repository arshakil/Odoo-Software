Annual/Earn Leave Management

** Customise The Earn Leave Management Module, Please Follow the steps below. **

Step 1:

Install Earn Leave Management Module

Click on leaves menu which is available on the top of the menu bar. after that look at left side and under the menu items Configuration Earn Leaves Calculation Click it.
  1. Assign only one specific integer value such as 18,20,22,30. by default given 18th days that means employee will get 1 day Earn Leave if employee passes 18 days from his/her joining date. Please edit depends on your company rules and regulations. don't create any new integer field

Step 2:

Click Configuration menu, Here you will find Leave types Earn Leaves So you don't need to create any type for Earn Leaves. Please

  1. Don't create Earn Leaves again. keep it as same as.
  2. No need to any leaves allocation for Earn Leaves, it will calculate leaves dynamically, depends on employees joining date.

Step 3:

Click on Employee menu items on top bar. then click on pages Personal Information. below will find Joining Date

Step 4:

Apply for earn leaves, Click on Leaves top menu bar, and you will find left side of menu items My Leaves--> Leaves Request click on it.

  1. In Form view select employee and leave type. actually, which type of leave we want? In our case Earn leaves. Select Earn Leave, it will show employee remaining days from his/her Earn Leaves.
